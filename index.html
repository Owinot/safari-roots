Our Story

A Journey Across
the Continent

Safari Roots was born from a simple conviction: that African food is among the world's most vibrant, complex, and soul-nourishing cuisines — and it deserves a seat at every table.

From the slow-braised stews of West Africa to the spiced grills of East Africa, our chefs bring decades of heritage and creativity to every dish.
